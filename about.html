— My Story

The eye
behind it all

Hey — I'm Al Furman, and I've been pointing cameras at the world for as long as I can remember. It started on the streets — just me, my camera, and a city full of stories nobody was stopping to notice. I fell in love with the idea that a single frame could hold an entire feeling.

Street photography is still where my heart lives. There's nothing quite like catching a stranger mid-laugh, or finding the perfect shadow cutting across a sidewalk at 7am. But over the years I've taken that same eye into landscapes, weddings, and events — anywhere life is happening and worth remembering.

I believe great photography shouldn't be out of reach. Whatever your budget, I'll work with you. The story matters more than the price tag.
