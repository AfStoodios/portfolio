Common questions

How does pricing work if I have a tight budget? +
Just reach out and be honest with me. I'd rather find a way to make it work than have you miss out on photos that matter to you. We can adjust scope, hours, or deliverables to fit what you can spend.
How long does it take to receive my photos? +
Typically 1–2 weeks for most shoots. Weddings and larger events may take up to 3 weeks. I always share a sneak peek within 48 hours so you have something to smile at while you wait.
Do you travel for shoots? +
Absolutely. Travel costs are discussed upfront and worked into the overall package so there are no surprises. Get in touch and let me know where you're thinking.
Can I purchase prints of my photos? +
Yes — fine art print options are available for all shoots. I work with quality print labs and can help you choose the right size and finish for your space.
